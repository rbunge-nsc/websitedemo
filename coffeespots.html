Seattle Essential Coffee Spots!

There are many coffee places in Seattle. Each coffee place has its uniqueness! I will introduce some of my favorite coffee spots that I highly recommend.

No.1 Starbucks Reserve Roastery

It is located in downtown Seattle.

Starbucks Reserve Roastery is absolutelly different from other regular starbuck stores. They serve not only coffee but also cocktails and alcoholic beverage at the bar.

No.2 Solstice Cafe

Located in University District.

Offering variety of coffee and drinks including a huge selection of tea.

No.3 Zoka Coffee Roaster & Tea Company

Locations: Greenlake, University District, South Lake Union, and Kirkland

Zoka Coffee has won the National Barista Championship in 2002 and 2002. They roast coffee in small batches, each weekday at their own roastery in Bellingham, so you can enjoy freshly roasted coffee anytime.
